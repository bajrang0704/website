IBRAHIMPATNAM

Ibrahimpatnam is a town and a municipality in Rangareddy district, Telangana state, India. It is located about 40 kilometers from Hyderabad. The town is known for its rich history and cultural heritage, with several ancient temples and monuments located in the area. In recent years, it has emerged as a rapidly growing suburb of Hyderabad. The economy of the town is largely based on agriculture and real estate development.
